Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a recipe for scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Turn stove on medium heat
  2. Crack eggs in to bowl.
  3. Add onion powder and a pinch of salt.
  4. Whisk eggs thoroughly until completely mixed.
  5. Place butter into skillet and move around whole skillet.
  6. Place eggs into skillet and let sit until you notice the edges start to form.
  7. Get spatula and start to move edges to the center until eggs start to form and scramble.
  8. Remove eggs from skillet on to plate once they've become the consistency and texture you want.
  9. Add pepper and a little more salt if you desire.
