Ramen Noodles

Description

This is a recipe for an amazing bowl of ramen noodles.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodle pack
  • Onion powder
  • Water

Steps

  1. Open ramen noodles package and break noodles in to 4 even pieces in to bowl.
  2. Pour just enough water in to bowl until noodles start to float.
  3. Place bowl of noodles in to microwave for 3 minutes.
  4. Take noodles out and strain water with just a little water remaining in bowl.
  5. Add packet seasoning and just a little bit of onion powder and stir with fork.
