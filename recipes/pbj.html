PBJ Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Place slices of bread on plate.
  2. Get spoon or knife.
  3. Spread peanut butter on to one slice of bread.
  4. Spread jelly on to other slice of bread.
  5. Combine slices of bread togther.
